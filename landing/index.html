FAQ

Questions owners ask us

Do my customers need to install an app?
No. The table QR opens the menu directly in their phone's browser — any phone, no install, no signup. Ordering takes under 90 seconds. Repeat diners can optionally create an account to keep their history, and native apps are on our roadmap.
Where does the money go when a customer pays?
Directly into your own wallet merchant account (ZAAD, EVC Plus, or Sahal). TableTap never touches or holds your revenue — our per-order fee is tallied separately and billed monthly with your subscription.
What do I need to get started?
A wallet merchant account (we walk you through the paperwork during onboarding), any tablet or phone for staff, and a printer for your table QR codes. From signup to your first live paid order typically takes under an hour once your merchant credentials are in hand.
I run a counter-service café with no waiters — does this work for me?
Yes. Switch your venue to pickup mode: customers order and pay from the QR, and get notified on their phone when the order is ready to collect at the counter. No waiter accounts needed.
Does it handle cash?
Fully. The cashier POS takes cash orders with a change calculator, and cash and wallet sales sit side by side in your shift totals and reports. Cash orders carry no per-order fee.
Which languages does it support?
Somali, Arabic, and English across the platform, with correct right-to-left layout for Arabic — your customer menu follows your venue's language. USD and Ethiopian Birr are both supported.
